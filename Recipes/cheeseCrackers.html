Spicy Garlic Cheese Crackers

Description

What pairs well with an ice cold glass of Strawberry Lemonade? Crackers do! But not just any kind cracker, oh no. What is missing now is something crunchy, savory, and salty. A spicy garlic cheese cracker is now the perfect summer snack. It is light, simple and flavorful like any great snack. Now lets get cruching.

Ingredients

  • 2 1/2 cups of shredded Sharp Cheese
  • 2 cups of shredded Pepper Jack Cheese
  • 1 cup of butter, cut into small pieces
  • 7 cups of water, divided
  • 1 1/2 cups of garbanzo bean flour
  • 1/2 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon of garlic
  • 1/2 teaspoon of cayenne pepper
  • 1/2 teaspoon of kosher salt

Steps

  1. Preheat overn to 375° F (190° C).
  2. Place chesse and butter in a bowl. Cover and let sit until it is room temp.
  3. Combine seasonings and flour in a seperate bowl.
  4. Mix cheese and butter together with your hands. Slowly add flour to cheese mixture until dough holds together in a ball. Roll dough onto a work surface, about 1/8 inch thick.
  5. Cut dough into cracker shapes using cutters or a knife
  6. Arrange dough pieces on a baking sheet.
  7. Bake in the preheated oven until crackers are crisp, 15 - 18 minutes.
