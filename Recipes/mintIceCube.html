Mint Ice Cubes

Description

Maybe you just want to get fancy. Maybe you just enjoy truly enjoy the taste. This mint ice cube recipe is sure to satisfiy that fancy flavor you are looking for. You can even get extravagant and subsititute the mint for another herb of your choosing. Whatever your flavor is, this ice cube recipe with surely get you there.

Ingredients

  • 6 grams of fresh mint leaves
  • 2 cups of boiling water

Steps

  1. Place leaves in boiling water for 3 minutes.
  2. Let water cool to room tempature
  3. Pour water in each ice cube tray. Be sure to place a leave in each cube.
  4. Freeze water until solid ice cubes.
