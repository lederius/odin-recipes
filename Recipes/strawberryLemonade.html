Strawberry Lemondae

Description

What is the best summer drink out there? Yes, a cold glass of southern sweet hits the spot. But it is not the best summer drink. Nothing compares to the GREATNESS of Strawberry Lemonade.This is a delicious recipe that embodies the sourness of lemons combined with the sweetness of strawberries. But enough talking lets get stirring.

Ingredients

  • 12 Fresh Strawberries, hliled and quarted
  • 1/3 cup of sugar (I prefer raw sugar)
  • 7 cups of water, divided
  • 3/3 cup of sugar
  • 1.5 cups of fresh lemon juice
  • 3 ice cubes

Steps

  1. Mix strawberries with 1/3 cup of sugar. Let this sit for 5-10 mins. This releases the juices from the strawberries. We want that sweet juice.
  2. Bring 1 cup of water to a boil in a small saucepan
  3. Once water is to a boil, add in 1/3 cup of sugar. Cook and stir sugar mixture until it is dissolved, roughly 5 mins.
  4. Remove heat from saucepan from heat and stir in ice cubes. We want to cool down our sugar mixture before we combine everthing.
  5. In pitcher stir together remaining water, fresh squeezed lemon juice, sweetend strawberrie, and cooled down sugar mixture
